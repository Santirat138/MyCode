Maximum product of a triplet (subsequence of size 3) in array
Given an integer array, find a maximum product of a triplet in the array.

Examples: 

Input:  [10, 3, 5, 6, 20]
Output: 1200
Explanation: Multiplication of 10, 6 and 20



Input:  [-10, -3, -5, -6, -20]
Output: -90



Input:  [1, -4, 3, -6, 7, 0]
Output: 168
